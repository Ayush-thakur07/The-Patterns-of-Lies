# Project Overview: Detective AI - The Patterns of Lies

## Introduction
**The Patterns of Lies** is an immersive, AI-driven detective investigation game. Players take on the role of a lead investigator tasked with solving complex cases by interrogating suspects, analyzing physical evidence, and connecting the dots on a deduction board.

The project features two primary cases:
- **Case DAI-006: The Midnight Curator**
- **Case DAI-007: The Daisy and the Fibonacci**

## Core Gameplay
- **Interrogation:** Engage in real-time, natural language conversations with suspects and witnesses powered by Google ADK and Gemini 2.5 Flash.
- **Evidence Analysis:** Examine high-resolution photos of physical evidence (e.g., a poisoned teacup, an origami crane) to uncover clues.
- **Deduction Board:** Organize findings and link suspects to motives and means.
- **Scene Navigation:** Move between locations such as the Detective's Room, the Sanctuary, and Interrogation Rooms.

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React with Vite for a fast, responsive UI.
- **Styling:** Vanilla CSS for custom, high-fidelity visuals and Framer Motion for animations.
- **AI Backend:** Google ADK (Agent Development Kit) utilizing Gemini 2.5 Flash for NPC logic and dialogue.
- **Asset Management:** Static assets (images, audio) served via the `public` directory.

## Current Cases
### DAI-006: The Midnight Curator
A high-stakes mystery set in a private gallery where the curator has been found dead.
- **Key Suspects:** Marcus Chen (Assistant Curator), Father Moretti, Sophie Lin.

### DAI-007: The Daisy and the Fibonacci
A botanical mystery where a scientist is found dead in a high-tech glass sanctuary.
- **Key Suspects:** Dr. Diane Park, Kye Harlan, and others.
